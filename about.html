About Me

Hello! I'm an aspiring UX designer and researcher based in Philadelphia. I am in my final year at Drexel University studying Computer Science and User Experience Interaction Design with a minor in Management Information Systems.

I know that might sound like a lot, but basically, I started my education in Computer Science and worked as a backend software engineer for a little over 2 years before I fell in love with the UX design process and seeing how my designs can impact users on a more personal level. With my background in technology, design, and management, I bring an interdisciplinary perspective to my work, and would love to talk more about my passions and projects with anyone who is interested!
